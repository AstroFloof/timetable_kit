Montreal-Trudeau Airport Shuttle:{{ br_or_space(key_on_right) }}{# #}VIA offers a free bus shuttle between Dorval railway station{{ br_or_space(key_on_right) }}{# #}and Montreal-Trudeau Airport in both directions.
{# #}It departs every 30 to 60 minutes from 7 AM to 11 PM.{{ br_or_space(key_on_right) }}{# #}The trip takes approximately 20 minutes depending on traffic.
{# #}See {# #}https://www.viarail.ca/en/plan/intermodality/montreal-trudeau-shuttle{{ br_or_space(key_on_right) }}{# #}for details.

{# #}

Dorval: VIA and Exo stations are separate{{ br_or_space(key_on_right) }}{# #}but connected by non-wheelchair-accessible footpaths.
